◆ PaymentType

enum Nop.Plugin.Payments.PayPalSmartPaymentButtons.Domain.PaymentType
strong

Represents payment type enumeration

Valores de enumerações
Capture 

The merchant intends to capture payment immediately after the customer makes a payment.

Authorize 

The merchant intends to authorize a payment and place funds on hold after the customer makes a payment. Authorized payments are guaranteed for up to three days but are available to capture for up to 29 days. After the three-day honor period, the original authorized payment expires and you must re-authorize the payment.
