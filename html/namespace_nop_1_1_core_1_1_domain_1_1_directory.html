◆ RoundingType

enum Nop.Core.Domain.Directory.RoundingType
strong

Rounding type

Valores de enumerações
Rounding001 

Default rounding (Match.Round(num, 2))

Rounding005Up 

Prices are rounded up to the nearest multiple of 5 cents for sales ending in: 3¢ & 4¢ round to 5¢; and, 8¢ & 9¢ round to 10¢

Rounding005Down 

Prices are rounded down to the nearest multiple of 5 cents for sales ending in: 1¢ & 2¢ to 0¢; and, 6¢ & 7¢ to 5¢

Rounding01Up 

Round up to the nearest 10 cent value for sales ending in 5¢

Rounding01Down 

Round down to the nearest 10 cent value for sales ending in 5¢

Rounding05 

Sales ending in 1–24 cents round down to 0¢ Sales ending in 25–49 cents round up to 50¢ Sales ending in 51–74 cents round down to 50¢ Sales ending in 75–99 cents round up to the next whole dollar

Rounding1 

Sales ending in 1–49 cents round down to 0 Sales ending in 50–99 cents round up to the next whole dollar For example, Swedish Krona

Rounding1Up 

Sales ending in 1–99 cents round up to the next whole dollar
